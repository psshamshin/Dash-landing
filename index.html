Platform

Built on three pillars

Everything Dash does comes back to trust, flexibility, and convenience — for both sides of every rental.

🔒

Trust & Safety

The #1 concern with peer-to-peer rentals is scams and disputes. We've engineered every layer to eliminate them.

  • Secure payment holding — no direct transfers
  • ID & driving license verified before booking
  • Ratings & reviews on every transaction
  • Insurance coverage on every trip
⚡

Flexibility

Thailand's rental market runs on negotiation — like Facebook Marketplace, but actually safe and structured.

  • Price negotiation between owner & renter
  • Flexible deposit system
  • Short-term or long-term rentals
  • Owner controls their own schedule
📱

Convenience

From browsing to driving in minutes — the whole experience is designed for mobile, wherever you are in Thailand.

  • Mobile-first booking flow
  • In-app chat with car owner
  • Instant booking confirmation
  • Local payment methods — PromptPay & wallets
